Security Incident Response Guidelines

If an employee receives a suspicious email that may be phishing, the
employee must not click any links or download attachments.

Steps to follow: 1. Immediately report the email to the IT Security
team. 2. Forward the email to security@company.com. 3. Delete the email
after reporting it.

Lost Laptop Procedure: If a company laptop is lost or stolen, employees
must report the incident to IT within 2 hours.

The IT department will remotely disable access and revoke credentials.

Password Security: Employees must use multi-factor authentication (MFA)
for all company systems. Passwords must be at least 12 characters long
and should not be reused across systems.
