# Employee Leave Policy

## Annual Leave

All full-time employees are entitled to **24 days of annual leave per
year**.

Leave accrues monthly and must be approved by the employee's reporting
manager before being taken.

## Sick Leave

Employees may take **up to 10 days of paid sick leave annually** with
medical documentation.

If sick leave exceeds 3 consecutive days, a medical certificate is
required.

## Public Holidays

Employees are entitled to all officially recognized UAE public holidays.

## Carry Forward Policy

Employees may carry forward a maximum of **5 unused leave days** to the
next calendar year.

Any additional unused leave will expire at the end of the year.
